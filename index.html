Rules:

This game is to be played by two players or teams with an equal number of people
For every else, ther're multiple versions possible.
The most common one is explained below.
The first pick is random (rolled out by Team-leader),
from the 2nd ongoing it's one pick Team 0, next pick Team 1 and so on.
If one team can't answer right, the other get a shot at it.
As soon as the game-master logs in the question in for the according team,
you'll get points worth the value of your question added to your score.
But there is one limitation, so you're only allowed to pick questions
from whatever category you prefer, within the bracker of 100 or 200 of value
as soon as there're no more questions to this restriction left,
you're free to pick from the 300-500 bracket.

After winning, you can claim your reward!
Good luck everyone!
